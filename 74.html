english I think Anna Todd has drawn a lot of attention. Although there have been criticisms, her book has been very successful.

english Yes, there's always criticism, but I think that's a sign that people are really reading books and talking about them.

english I think one problem modern authors face is competition from mass media such as TV and movies. There are so many entertainment options and I think this can reduce interest in reading.

english I think it also depends on the society you grew up in. If you grow up in a reading culture, you're likely to be interested in it as well.
